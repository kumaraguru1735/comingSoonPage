Written by PolyMind AI Studio
Updated: 17 March 2024

Introduction:

At PolyMind AI Studio, we are committed to delivering a top-quality experience for our users. We understand that situations may arise where you may need to request a refund for your subscription. To ensure transparency and clarity, we have established the following refund policy:

Refund Eligibility:

  • Subscription Type: This refund policy applies to both weekly and yearly subscriptions.
  • Qualifying Reasons for Refund:
    • Major Faults: You are eligible for a refund if the app experiences significant faults, such as not functioning as described or being notably defective.
    • Technical Issues: You are eligible for a refund if persistent technical issues, beyond our control, impede access to the app.
    • Non-Delivery of image request: You are eligible for a refund if purchased image request are not delivered or credited to your account due to system errors.
    • Defective Digital Goods: You are eligible for a refund if purchased digital goods within the app are significantly defective or do not match their descriptions.

Refund Exceptions:

Refund requests made after the 30-minute timeframe will not be considered. Refunds will not be granted if 20 or more image request have been used within the subscription period. Refunds will not be granted for subscription renewals or subsequent billing cycles beyond the initial purchase.

Refund Amount:

For eligible refunds, the refund amount may be subject to reduction based on associated administrative costs and the duration of the subscription used.

Cancellation of Subscriptions:

Should you wish to cancel your subscription without seeking a refund, you can do so at any time by following this link or by contacting our customer support team. Canceling your subscription will prevent any future renewals or charges.

Providing Relevant Details for Refund Requests:

In order for us to efficiently process your refund requests, we kindly ask that users provide the following relevant details:

  • Proof of Purchase: Please include a copy of your purchase receipt or confirmation email containing details of your subscription purchase.
  • Subscription Type and Duration: Specify whether you have a weekly or yearly subscription and the duration of your subscription period.
  • Description of Issue: Clearly outline the reason for your refund request, including any major faults, misleading descriptions, technical issues, or non-delivery of image request experienced.
  • Token Usage: If applicable, provide information regarding the number of image request used within the subscription period, as refunds may not be granted if a certain threshold of token usage is exceeded.
  • Screenshots or Documentation: If possible, attach screenshots or other documentation illustrating the issue encountered within the product.

By providing these necessary details, our team can effectively investigate and address your refund requests in a timely manner, ensuring a satisfactory resolution for all parties involved.

Technical Issues and Support:

In the event of technical issues or difficulties with our service, please contact our customer support team for assistance. We will make every effort to address and resolve any problems you encounter promptly and efficiently.

Modification of Refund Policy:

We reserve the right to modify or update this refund policy at any time. The most recent version will be posted on our website.
