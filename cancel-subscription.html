How to Cancel Your Subscription on Android

Written by PolyMind AI Studio
Updated: 17/March/2024

While we're eager to assist you, canceling subscriptions for Android devices is managed through the Google Play Store. To cancel your subscription, please follow these steps:

  1. On your Android device, open the Google Play Store app.
  2. Tap on your profile icon located at the top right corner of the screen.
  3. Select "Payments & subscriptions" from the menu.
  4. Choose the subscription you wish to cancel from the list.
  5. Tap on "Cancel subscription" and follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the cancellation.

By following these steps, you'll be able to cancel your subscription seamlessly on your Android device.
